The Method & Quality

The Ritual & Standards

■ Materials & Assembly

Every single implement that leaves our Swedish atelier conforms to five essential physical milestones:

  • • Selected exclusively from the lower meter of slow-grown Nordic White Birch trunks for maximum fiber density.
  • • Dried naturally in a humidity-controlled atmosphere for 180 days to eliminate trace structural moisture.
  • • Shaved to a microscopic taper ranging between 0.35mm at the tip and 2.1mm at the base.
  • • Soaked via direct infusion containing zero synthetic carrier liquids, alcohol, or stabilizing compounds.
  • • Inspected by hand under magnifying optics before being sealed inside your protective metallic travel cylinder.

Ⅰ The Post-Dining Ritual

To extract the sensory value of our steeped botanical finishes, follow this four-stage sensory experience:

  1. 1 Gently slide open the anodized seal of your travel case and withdraw a single grain-aligned toothpick.
  2. 2 Hold the tip near the olfactory area for five seconds to release the cold-pressed peppermint or barrel peat notes.
  3. 3 Gently apply across the palate. The organic infusion is heat-activated by contact to refresh oral tissue.
  4. 4 Secure the threaded metallic cap onto your cylinder immediately to maintain ambient flavor integrity for remaining picks.
