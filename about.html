Our Story

Founded with a commitment to pure minimalism, Aureum redefines ordinary functional design. We don't believe in mass production. We believe in creating elegant, premium, and durable wooden toothpick collections for individuals who value detail.

Why Choose Aureum?

  • Splinter-free, hand-polished finishes
  • Completely organic with zero chemical coatings
  • Sleek, pocket-sized protective cases
